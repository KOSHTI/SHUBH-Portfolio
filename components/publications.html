Innovative Deep Learning Approach for Parkinson's Disease Prediction: Leveraging Convolutional Neural Networks for Early Detection

Shubham M. Koshti, et al.

EAI Endorsed Transactions on Pervasive Health and Technology(Sprtinger)

May 29, 2024

Parkinson Disease Healthy Control CNN MRI Scans Deep Learning

Developed an automated Parkinson's disease diagnostic system using convolutional neural networks on MRI images. Leveraging the PPMI dataset, pretrained models such as VGG16 and ResNet50 were used to distinguish PD patients from healthy controls. The system achieved high diagnostic accuracy and demonstrated strong capability in identifying early-stage Parkinson's disease.
